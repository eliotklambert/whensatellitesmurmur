Starlink Avian Services

July 2025. SpaceX headquarters. Mr Musk has just unveiled his most ambitious idea yet - Starlink Avian Services, the first satellite service in existence marketed just for birds. Although SpaceX has generated $10 billion in profit in just this year alone, Mr Musk wants to expand the service to an interspecies audience in a connectivity revolution.

(A conversation between Musk (EM) and a prospective avian customer (AC), August 1 2025)

EM: Hello prospective customer, Elon here calling you from Starlink Avian Services…

AC: sqqwwakwiaiia? (Erm what?)

EC: …I present to you Starlink’s most groundbreaking innovation yet - the first ever Avian satellite system designed to optimize your flocking coordination. Fully equipped with the most up to date weather news, migration routes, and inflight dining services, This isn’t just another product, but a revolution in connectivity. Here at Starlink, we believe that every species deserves to be connected, and our custom-built wearable antennas will ensure that you are! Are you still there?

AC to Mum of AC: Sksacawww wska (do you think it’s another scammer?)

EM: Remember that the early bird catches the worm! And you will be earlier than your peers by 10% if you optimize your migration routes through our satellite package.

AC: Mackawww Macaaaaw?! (who does he think we are, McDonald island penguins?!)

EM: Are you still interested? Are you there? Press 1 to begin the antenna installation process.

AC: FUCK OFF!

Mum of AC: Dffawwwwwk?! (Where did you learn that?!!)

AC: swarrrrk swarkkk (the kids at the park)

EM: Right. Your loss, prospective customer. Think of the optimization. Oh it makes me emotional just -

(AC Hangs up)

AC: cacaaaaw (Thy ambition, Thou scarlet sin, robb'd this bewailing land)

Mum of AC: Dffawwwwwk?! (Where did you learn that?!!)
